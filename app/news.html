Recent Meetings and Progress

  • Sponser Meeting 9/25/14

    We discussed the uses cases of the product and the general expectations of how it should work from the end user's perspective. We decided on a technology stack using: Docker, Python, PostgreSQL. These technologies will drive the backend data processing and will interface with other applications and the frontend client (that will be built later on) useing a RESTful API.

  • Group Meeting 9/23/14

    We discussed the inital research that was conducted about the project and the technologies that will be used to implement the requested features and algorithms. We debated the benefits of different programming languages; mainly focusing on Python and Java/Clojure for their better library support. We set some inital goals and hashed out potential and initial implementations.

  • Week of 9/15/14

    We had our first meeting with our sponser Ryan Raub. We got the inital details of the project and were introduced to the project topics and options. We scheduled to meet weekly while getting the project off the ground.
